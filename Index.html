Martel Industries

Founded by Raynald, Martel Industries remains a covert cornerstone of European defense innovation, operating through shadows to reshape the battlefield.

Origin

Formed during the aftermath of continental reconstruction, Martel began as a neutral armorer for broken nations. In the silence of old ruins, it built the future piece by piece. Raynald—visionary, recluse, architect— engineered its foundation, making each division self-sufficient, deniable, and precise.

Divisions & Current Operations

Applied Sciences Experimental materials, cybernetics, and human-environment interfaces tested under combat simulations.
Engineering Development of modular systems—armor, drones, surveillance arrays—all configured for disposal and remanufacture.
Global Surveillance Data funneling from satellites, AI pattern recognition, and orbital blindspot computation.
